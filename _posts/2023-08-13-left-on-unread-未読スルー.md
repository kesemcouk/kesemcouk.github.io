---
title: "Left on Unread"
subtitle: "未読スルー"
date: 2023-08-13
categories: 
  - "japan-two"
  - "poems"
---

What are you thinking? 
How are you feeling? 
Impossible to penetrate 
 
How did it turn so? 
What was the moment that ruined all? 
If I could go back 
 
Words on my tongue 
Speech on my lips 
Waiting to erupt 
Trapped within 
 
You lied 
 
What are you doing? 
Why are you playing? 
I wonder if it worked 
 
When will you make it clear? 
Where did your words disappear? 
Please don’t ever go 
 
Long, silent drives 
That awkward silence arrives 
"See you!" we both say 
Knowing we’ll never cross paths again 
 
You left 
 
Where is this mess going? 
When will I find you longing? 
At night, in my head 
 
Why don’t you share my thoughts? 
How can we be so lost? 
I will never understand 
 
The only thing worse than 
Being left on read 
Is not being read to begin with 
You said you always regretted things
