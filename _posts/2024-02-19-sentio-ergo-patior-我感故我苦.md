What do you want from me? 
Why don’t you tell me?  
I know you ache, but something’s holding you back 
 
Did you lie when you said those words? 
Vanished into my train of thought  
A ghost, materialized back 
 
Where did you fly me to? 
Bluffs on Venus, red and blue  
Left me stranded as you turned your back  
 
You made me soft in more than one sense  
Your obelisk eclipsed the rest  
Mentioned rings, promised sequels 
The story ended before it began 
 
I’m not happy for you 
Don’t talk to me about him 
He can be with you 
While all I have is memories  
 
So I sit and hold back tears  
I hear from you; you disappear  
I never riddle the human brain 
I laugh. Yet another gaffe
