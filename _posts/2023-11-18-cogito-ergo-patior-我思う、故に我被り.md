Foreign hands  
Dark lights 
I think of you 
And open my eyes  
 
Sneaking into your workplace  
Laughing on a staircase at 2 AM 
Giving you oxygen 
Brushing knees inside a train 
 
Do you think of me like I think of you  
Do you think of me at all? 
A video with no end 
A microphone with no voice 
 
Back-to-back dates  
I chew on my pencil 
Making out in a garden 
Chagrin, what a bargain 
 
Why do some get along, while others are strangers? 
Why do those who get along become strangers? 
How come one makes me yawn, 
While another builds a shelter? 
Oh, to read your mind, and understand your rancour 
 
If I could crack the code 
There would be no more disillusion 
Privy to my species 
Who is this homo sapiens 
 
Does it kill you that I’m out there 
Did you plan to disappear? 
You forgot, but I didn’t 
Time never heals  
 
On the same planet  
In a different galaxy 
A parallel universe  
Without Wi-Fi or electricity  
A kiss at a club  
A hot, hot spring  
An abandoned theme park  
I’ll keep imagining
