---
title: "For the Last Time"
subtitle: "最後に"
date: 2023-04-18
categories: 
  - "japan-one"
  - "poems"
---

Scorching water 
Minerals floating inside 
Open air and breezy 
Misty alps looming by 
Rocky pools 
Milky liquids, stinking sulphur 
Soaking in volcanic aftermaths 
Nature’s rare gift to us 
Raindrops dripping from above 
Refreshing and welcome 
No photos allowed 
Words are enough 
Bus crossing fog 
On the way to heaven 
Probably for the last time 
Probably forever
