---
title: "Not for Long"
subtitle: "長くはない"
date: 2023-02-27
categories: 
  - "japan-one"
  - "poems"
---

Pockets full of pen and paper 
I go out alone 
The wind is my sole companion 
The sun shines once more 
I think about things 
Snow wets my soles 
People make conversation 
Temperatures drop 
I am free 
But not for long 
And I wonder on which note  
To end this poem
